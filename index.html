PROJECTS/ INTERNSHIPS

  • [May-July 2023] Used statistical models and machine learning to make predictions on the optimal fertility rate of India. Created a shiny app entirely based on R statistical software that predicts the population of India based on the available data on migration rate, death rate, fertility rate and population of India, and also calculates the optimal fertility rate given a target population in the future for India. The application is available at: https://palash.shinyapps.io/Fertility_rate/. My advisor was Dr. Palash Ghosh of IIT Guwahati.
  • [November-December 2022] Project titled Continued fractions and Pell's equation, under Dr. Rupam Barman of IIT Guwahati. The project notes can be accessed here.
